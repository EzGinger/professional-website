About Me

My name is Curtis Jones and I am a son, a brother, a friend, and a cybersecurity enthusist. I am a hard-working individual but a more dedicated team member. I have grown up in San Diego my entire life and have always had a passion for technology and sports. I played football from the time I was 7 years old to my final season, my senior year at Grossmont High School. As a senior a made many great networks through the help of my wonderful English for Business teacher and the Junior Acheivements Ambassador Program. There I learned the importance of finance and networking, and the importance of marketing yourself. I have since been an avid investor in the stock market since 2017, day-trading, swing-trading, and managing my own ROTH IRA. With my interest in stocks and tech, I found an interest in cybersecurity, attacks started popping up in the news headlines more often and I wanted to learn more.

How it all Began

My passion for technology truly grew in my first year of college when I took my first CS class at Grossmont-Cuyamacca Community College. There I was introduced to the head of the IT department that allowed be to dive deep into MIS/CS classes and to learn about cybersecurity. Since that year, I have progressed my career in computer science and information systems exponentially. I first began by learning about some of the base principles of cybersecurity such as the CIA triad and good practices. I then went on to learn about python and educated myself about the linux command line and started working with virtual machines on OracleVMBox my sophmore year of college. After building my first PC I installed my first instance of Kali Linux and began working through some of the most popular applications utilized on Kali Linux through an amazing web application, TryHackMe.com.
